Let’s work together

We work closely with our community partners to develop referral programs and a coordinated, cooperative approach to providing access to justice.

Here’s how we can work together

Referral programs

Start a referral program between our organisations to ensure our clients get the most appropriate service for their needs

Cross-organisational training

Share relevant trainings and resources to efficiently build capacity in our people

Partner on projects

Collaborate on transformational projects which align with our organisations’ strategic direction

Meet our community partners

We gratefully acknowledge the community partners who have worked alongside Anika Legal to grow our collective impact.

Want to work together?

By working cooperatively with other community organisations we can support each other and amplify our strengths. That means more appropriate, efficient services and fewer people slipping through the cracks.

Here are a handful of ways you can partner with us:

  • Referral programs
  • Cross-organisational training
  • Partner on projects

If you’d like to learn more about how your community organisation can work with Anika, please get in touch with our Community Partnerships team.
