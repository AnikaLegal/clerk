Volunteer at Anika

The hands-on work we do at Anika means real-world experience while providing the opportunity to escape from the structure of traditional companies.

With a strong mentorship culture across the organisation and through external partnerships, Anika is a place for people enthusiastic to learn, establish meaningful relationships, and make a difference.

Why join us?

Meaningful work

Our work makes a difference and helps those who need it most. If you are excited by the prospect of building something from the ground up through hands-on, high impact work – we want to hear from you!

Work in a driven, innovative team

Become a part of a vibrant culture, and a close-knit team passionate about making a difference in the world. Staying true to our innovative vision, we pride ourselves on doing things differently in the legal industry.

Personal & professional development‍

As a rapidly growing organisation, we are constantly looking for people eager to upskill in areas they are passionate about. If you want to learn something, we want to know about it. We grow Anika’s impact by growing our volunteers.
