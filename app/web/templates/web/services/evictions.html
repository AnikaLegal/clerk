Getting evicted? Know your rights before you panic!

Landlords can now evict tenants who have fallen behind on their rent.

Received a notice to vacate? There’s no time to waste. Get free, ongoing legal support within 2 working days.

What does it involve?

Start your case with us and you’ll receive free legal assistance and advice that is:

  • Specific to your circumstances
  • Online and easy to understand
  • Within 2 working days of starting your case

Whether it’s negotiating a payment plan with your landlord on your behalf or buying you some time in your home to find a new place, we’ve got you covered.

Am I eligible?

You are eligible for this service if you’re a renter living in Victoria who is being evicted because you are behind on rent payments.

Still not sure if you're eligible? Submit your case, and we’ll let you know.

Here’s the step-by-step breakdown

1. Complete our questionnaire

It is fully online and takes less than 10 minutes

2. We’ll call you

One of our friendly team will call you within two working days to discuss your situation

3. Receive your personalised legal advice

You’ll get an email outlining your rights and options

4. Negotiate a payment plan

We can help you negotiate a payment plan for unpaid rent with your landlord

5. Reach a resolution at VCAT

We’ll help you prepare for your VCAT hearing, whether it’s to stop the eviction process, agree on a payment plan, or ask for one more month in your rental to look for your next accommodation
