Getting evicted? Know your rights before you panic!

Is your landlord trying to evict you because you exercised your rights? Perhaps you asked for repairs, or complained about a rent increase. We are here to help.

Received a retaliatory notice to vacate? There's no time to waste. Get free, ongoing legal support.

What does it involve?

Start your case with us and you'll receive free legal assistance and advice that is:

  • Specific to your circumstances
  • Online and easy to understand

Whether it's buying you some time in your home to find a new place, or preparing you to challenge the eviction at VCAT, we've got you covered.

Am I eligible?

You are eligible for this service if you're a renter living in Victoria who is being evicted because you stood up for your rental rights.

Still not sure if you're eligible? Submit your case, and we'll let you know.

Here's the step-by-step breakdown

1. Complete our questionnaire

It is fully online and takes less than 10 minutes

2. We'll call you

One of our friendly team will call you to discuss your situation

3. Receive your personalised legal advice

You'll get an email outlining your rights and options

4. We may negotiate with your landlord

We can contact your Rental Provider or Agent to try and negotiate a resolution or ask that they give you more time while you look for your next rental

5. Reach a resolution at VCAT

We'll help you prepare for your VCAT hearing, whether it's to stop the eviction process, or ask for one more month in your rental to look for your next accommodation
