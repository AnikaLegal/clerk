Refer someone to Anika

Anika provides free legal advice and ongoing casework to vulnerable renters who are being evicted for rental arrears, or who need repairs performed in their rental.

You can refer someone to Anika Legal’s services by helping them fill out this 10 minute questionnaire, or sending an email to referrals@anikalegal.com.

What type of cases can
we take?

Eviction for rental arrears

We can help Victorian renters who have received a notice to vacate for rental arrears, and a possession order has not yet been granted.

Repairs

We can help Victorian renters who require urgent or non-urgent repairs in their rental property.

What will we do?

1. Legal advice

We’ll provide legal advice outlining our client’s rights and options

2. Casework

We’ll communicate with their landlord on our client’s behalf, and apply for VCAT if necessary

3. VCAT preparation

We’ll work with our client to prepare them to self-represent at VCAT

How can I refer to Anika?

Intake questionnaire

The best way to refer a client is to help them complete our 10-minute questionnaire.

Email us

If you already have written information about your client’s case you can email their contact details and circumstances to referrals@anikalegal.com

Call us

If you are unsure if Anika is appropriate for your client, leave us a message on 0480 016 398 and we will call you back within two business days.

Who can we help?

We can help people who:

  • Rent a property in Victoria and are on the lease
  • Understand basic English (interpreters available)
  • Have access to a smartphone or computer
  • Does not require physical representation at VCAT

99% of clients referred to Anika meet our eligibility criteria. We conduct an eligibility assessment after receiving the questionnaire submission, and on the rare occasion a client does not meet the criteria, we refer them to the most appropriate service.

How can my organisation refer to Anika?

If your community organisation would like to discuss the best way to refer clients to Anika, our Community Partnerships Team can provide your organisation with up to date referral resources or present at your team meeting. Email us at partnerships@anikalegal.com
